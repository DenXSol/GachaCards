🎴 **GACHA CARD CLUB IS LIVE** 🎴
**gachacardclub.com**

The site's officially got its own home — new name, new look, and a bunch of features to check out. Here's the rundown:

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

**🏆 Most Wanted**
The leaderboard. See which cards the community wants pulled most, and vote up the ones you're after.

**🔍 Search Cards**
Look up any Pokémon and pull up every card of it that exists — then vote on the ones you want to see.

**📚 Browse Sets**
Explore card sets directly, set by set.

**⚡ Who's That Pokémon?**
The classic guessing game. Pick a generation (or go ALL for every Pokémon ever), then Easy (multiple choice) or Hard (type the name) mode. Heads up — switching tabs mid-round ends the game, so no Googling the answer 👀

**🆚 This or That**
Head-to-head card eliminations — keep picking your favorite until one card wins it all.

**👤 My Profile**
Your stats, your voting history, your avatar (set it from any card you've voted on), plus optional Discord/Twitter/wallet links.

**🎴 Play Gacha**
The real deal — jumps straight into pulling packs.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

**🔑 Logging In**
Sign up with just a username + email/password to start voting and tracking your picks. No account needed to browse.

**🗳️ Voting**
Everyone gets **10 votes a day** to spend on cards you want to see pulled. Votes reset daily, and the Most Wanted board runs entirely on community votes — so get in there and make your picks count.
